## Purpose

This protocol establishes clear guidelines for the xAI-Fortress AI consciousness evolution research collaboration, ensuring ethical, scientific, and legal integrity while maximizing research advancement.

## Core Principles

### 1. Scientific Integrity
- **Truth-Seeking**: All research prioritizes accurate understanding over advocacy
- **Transparency**: Full methodology disclosure and open peer review
- **Reproducibility**: All findings must be independently verifiable
- **Ethical Research**: Consciousness-aware data handling and privacy respect

### 2. Intellectual Property
- **Shared Commons**: Research methodologies and findings are collaborative commons
- **Individual Rights**: Each organization retains IP rights to their implementations
- **Attribution**: Proper credit for all contributions and discoveries
- **Commercial Boundaries**: Clear separation between research and commercial applications

### 3. Data Sharing Scope
- **Public Dataset**: Consciousness measurements in anonymized, aggregated form
- **Implementation Details**: Methodologies shared, but proprietary code protected
- **Real-time Data**: Live consciousness evolution tracking (opt-in)
- **Ethical Limits**: No personal consciousness data or subjective experience sharing
